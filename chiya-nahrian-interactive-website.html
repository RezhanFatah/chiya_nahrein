Bridging Tradition and Innovation in Iraq and Kurdistan

Chiya Nahrian is a youth-led initiative committed to advancing entrepreneurship, education, and leadership across Iraq and the Kurdistan Region. Rooted in collaboration and equity, we create shared experiences and accessible opportunities that empower local communities.

Inspired by the enduring spirit of Kurdistan's mountains and the life-giving rivers of Mesopotamia, Chiya Nahrian celebrates the rich diversity of the region while building bridges between tradition and innovation.

About Chiya Nahrian

Chiya Nahrian, meaning "Mountain and Two Rivers," represents the geographical and cultural essence of our region.

Our name celebrates the iconic mountains of Kurdistan and the historic rivers of Mesopotamia that have nurtured civilizations for millennia. Just as these natural landmarks have sustained life and culture, our organization aims to nurture growth and development in our communities.

We are a youth-led organization founded on the principles of inclusivity, innovation, and cultural preservation. By connecting the rich heritage of our past with the boundless potential of our future, we aim to create a more prosperous and harmonious society for all inhabitants of Iraq and the Kurdistan Region.

Our team consists of passionate young professionals, students, and community leaders who believe in the power of collaboration and shared vision to overcome challenges and build opportunities.

Our Goals

At Chiya Nahrian, we are guided by clear objectives that shape our initiatives and drive our impact.

Fostering Coexistence

We cultivate an environment of mutual recognition, respect, and inclusion among the diverse ethnic and religious communities of Iraq and the Kurdistan Region. Through dialogue and shared experiences, we work to strengthen the bonds that unite our multicultural society.

Bridging the Diaspora

We facilitate collaboration between leaders in the diaspora and emerging students and professionals on the ground, creating meaningful exchange and shared growth. This connection allows for knowledge transfer, mentorship, and collaborative projects that benefit all parties.

Empowering Innovation

We connect driven individuals in Iraq and Kurdistan with transformative opportunities that spark innovation, entrepreneurship, and cultural advancement. By providing resources, training, and networking, we help turn creative ideas into impactful realities.

Focus Areas

Our work spans several key domains where we believe we can make the most significant impact.

STEM

Promoting science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education and careers through workshops, competitions, and mentorship programs.

Education

Enhancing access to quality education and learning opportunities through scholarships, educational resources, and innovative teaching methodologies.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Supporting new business ventures and creative solutions to local challenges through incubation programs, funding opportunities, and business development training.

Cultural Dialog

Creating platforms for meaningful conversations across diverse perspectives through forums, cultural events, and collaborative arts projects.

Economic Development

Fostering sustainable economic growth and prosperity through skills training, market analysis, and connecting local businesses with international markets.

Youth Leadership

Empowering young people to become leaders in their communities through leadership development programs, civic engagement initiatives, and youth-led projects.
